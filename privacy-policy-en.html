1. Introduction

Welcome to the "News Reader" application (the "App"). We take your privacy seriously. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and protect your personal information. By using this App, you agree to the terms of this Privacy Policy.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Information We Collect

This App is committed to protecting your privacy. We collect very limited information:

  • Device Information: We may collect anonymous technical information such as device model and OS version to provide better service
  • Usage Data: Anonymous statistics about app usage and feature usage frequency
  • Network Information: Network connection status (to check network availability)

2.2 Information We Do NOT Collect

Important: This App does NOT collect the following information:

  • Personal identification information (name, email, phone number, etc.)
  • Location information
  • Contact information
  • Device identifiers (such as IMEI, MAC address, etc.)
  • Payment information
  • Any sensitive personal information

3. How We Use Information

We use the collected information only for the following purposes:

  • To provide and improve app functionality
  • To optimize user experience
  • To diagnose and resolve technical issues
  • To ensure secure and stable app operation

We do NOT:

  • Sell your information to third parties
  • Use your information for advertising purposes
  • Share your personal information without your consent

4. Third-Party Services

4.1 NewsAPI.org

This App uses news data services provided by NewsAPI.org. When you browse news in this App, your device connects directly to NewsAPI.org servers to fetch news content.

Data Transmission:

  • All data transmission is encrypted via HTTPS protocol
  • We do not pass your personal information to NewsAPI.org
  • NewsAPI.org may log your IP address (standard network service behavior)

For NewsAPI.org's privacy policy, please visit: https://newsapi.org/privacy

4.2 Image Loading

News images may come from multiple news source websites. Image loading is done via HTTPS protocol, and we do not collect or store these images.

5. Data Storage

Local Storage: This App does not persistently store personal information on your device. The App may use local device storage to cache news data for faster loading, and this data is stored only on your device.

Server Storage: We do not operate servers to store your personal information. All news data is fetched in real-time from third-party services (NewsAPI.org).

6. Data Security

We take the following measures to protect your information security:

  • Use HTTPS encryption for all network data transmission
  • App code is obfuscated to enhance security
  • Follow Android platform security best practices
  • Regularly update the App to fix security vulnerabilities

7. Data Sharing

We do NOT share your personal information with third parties, except when:

  • Required by laws and regulations
  • To protect our rights and property
  • To prevent fraud or security threats

8. Your Rights

Under applicable data protection laws, you have the following rights:

  • Right to Access: Know what information we have collected about you
  • Right to Deletion: Request deletion of your information (if applicable)
  • Right to Withdraw Consent: Withdraw your consent to data processing at any time
  • Right to Stop Using: Uninstall the App and stop using it at any time

Since this App does not collect personally identifiable information, most data protection rights may not apply. If you have any questions, please contact us using the information below.

9. Children's Privacy

This App is not directed to children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us immediately.

10. Privacy Policy Changes

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. For significant changes, we will notify you within the App and update the "Last Updated" date at the top of this page. We recommend that you review this Privacy Policy periodically to stay informed of the latest information.

11. Scope of Application

This Privacy Policy applies to the "News Reader" App downloaded and used through the Google Play Store. This policy does not apply to App versions obtained through other means or third-party websites.
